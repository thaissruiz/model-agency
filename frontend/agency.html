Our history

Founded in 1995 by businessman Eli Hadid Wahbe, the Thoma`s Model agency holds the title of the first and only multinational of Brazilian origin in the segment. The agency, which has been in the market for more than 20 years and is responsible for closing numerous advertising campaigns, national and international fashion shows, presents a casting made up of models recognized both in Brazil and abroad.

Eli was responsible for launching some of the most influential women in the country, such as Ana Paula Arósio, Luana Piovani, Fernanda Lima and Taís Araújo. The businessman was also responsible for creating the Menina Fantástico contest, shown in 2012 on Rede Globo's “Fantástico” program.

Along with this contest, there are the biggest beauty and fashion events in Brazil, such as the Tic Tac Mega Model, Palmolive Mega Model, Dream Fashion Tour and, more recently, Atroveran Mega Model, which offered more than R $ 1 million in awards and toured more than 300 cities across the country looking for a new star.
